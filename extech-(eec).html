About

Founded 1978

eec (Extech Electronics) is the first variable frequency power supply manufacturer in Taiwan, and is the first ever test instrument brand awarded with TUV/GS safety certification. In 2003, eec has joined to the global leading safety brand Ikonix in recognition of its excellent products, services, and innovations.

 

Over the past thirty years, eec has released products that are being awarded among the best. For example, the 7-in-1 TFT LCD composite analyzer ESA has won the Taiwan Excellence Award in 2012 and the capacitive-type touch-screen tester SE was the winner of Taiwan Excellence Award in 2015. eec launched its Quality Extension Plan in 2011, moving one step ahead of its competitors to extend warranty periods (3 years for flagship products and 2 years for general products). With the Quality Extension Plan, eec becomes the most reliable choice for customers.
